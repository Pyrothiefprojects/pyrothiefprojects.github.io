About

Project Nova is a multi-genre game framework built from custom engines, each powering a Nova title and serving as a reusable dev kit. It comprises four interconnected stories and a shared hub world, all set in the Nova Universe.

The four titles:

  • A spaceship combat roguelike
  • A browser-based point-and-click adventure
  • A squadron-based space dogfighter
  • A survival exploration title

All titles connect through Club Nova — a shared hub where players track their progress, turn in rewards, and cross paths with characters from across the realm.

The goal is to ship all four games as finished products while also releasing the engines behind them — giving players and creators the tools to build their own content in the genres each engine specializes in.
